Alide (Aliide)

Alide's parents Madis and Maarja Noone originated from Estonia. Like many others they migrated across an open border to neighbouring Russia to farm land near the town of Gatchina, a satellite town of St Petersburg.

My father clearly recalled his mother reminiscing about her stay across the border in Estonia. Alide stayed with family in Estonia whilst she developed skills as a seamstress. Albert was able to recall his mother's wish to return to Estonia.
